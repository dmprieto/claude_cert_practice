Given the following description, write a well-structured engineering ticket.

Format:
## Title
One line, imperative mood (e.g. "Add rate limiting to /api/search endpoint").

## Background
1–2 sentences on why this work is needed.

## Acceptance Criteria
Bullet list of testable outcomes. Each item starts with "Given/When/Then" or a clear measurable condition.

## Out of Scope
What this ticket explicitly does NOT cover.

## Notes
Any implementation hints, risks, or links to related work.
